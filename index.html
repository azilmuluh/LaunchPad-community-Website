About LaunchPad

The Story of LaunchPad Community

LaunchPad Community began as a simple but purposeful initiative. Initially created as a small orientation group to support participants of the International Youth Math Challenge (IYMC). What started as separate support spaces evolved into one unified, growing community, driven by a shared need: access to opportunities, clarity, and support.

Founded on December 5th, 2025, LaunchPad Community is a youth-led ecosystem based in Cameroon designed to help young people truly change their lives through timely access to opportunities, guidance and follow-up, and a safe space to grow, network, and collaborate.

As the group grew, it naturally attracted other motivated students hungry for guidance and opportunities. Soon after, this group collaborated with and later merged into an ICSC-oriented group, bringing together learners from different academic and competitive backgrounds.

The inspiration behind LaunchPad is deeply personal. As a secondary school student, our Founder and CEO often felt disconnected from the vast opportunities available globally. Even when some opportunities became visible, self-doubt, lack of guidance, and the feeling that they were "too far-reaching" or "unrealistic" often held him back.

Everything changed after joining Open Dreams Educational NGO. Within a short period, he gained access to the right circles, mentors, and information.
